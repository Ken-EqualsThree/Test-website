Our story

We believe neighbors
should help neighbors.

Etion started with a simple idea: that every community has more than enough talent, time, and goodwill to take care of itself — it just needs a place to meet.

Our mission

Community
over transaction

Etion isn't a gig platform. There are no background fees, no corporate extraction. It's a collective — the value stays in the community.

We designed Etion around credits, not cash, so helping your neighbor isn't transactional. You give time, you earn time. You build trust, you build community.

What we stand for

Our principles

Four values that guide every decision we make — from product design to community governance.

🤝
Reciprocity

Help flows both ways. Everyone gives and everyone receives. No one is just a customer, and no one is just a worker.

🏘️
Locality

Real community is hyper-local. We're built for your street, your block, your neighborhood — not the entire internet.

🔒
Trust-first

Trust is earned through reputation, not purchased through verification badges. Our rating system is built by the community, for the community.

⚖️
Fairness

We take no cut from community exchanges. Etion is sustained through optional memberships, not extraction from every transaction.

How we got here

The story
so far

Etion started in 2022 as a WhatsApp group in Amsterdam's Jordaan neighborhood. Three friends — tired of expensive handyman apps and lonely city living — started asking their neighbors for help directly.

The response was overwhelming. Within a month, 40 households were exchanging help: cooking meals, carrying furniture, watching plants, fixing bikes. The credit system emerged organically — a way to keep things fair without involving money.

By 2023, neighboring districts asked to join. By 2024, Etion became a proper platform. Today, 47 neighborhoods across the Netherlands run on Etion, and we're just getting started.
