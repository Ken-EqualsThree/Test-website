How it works

Help flows
both ways

Every member is both a helper and someone who gets helped. The credit system keeps it fair without making it transactional.

1
Join your neighborhood

Sign up and get matched to your local Etion neighborhood — the people living within a few blocks of you.

2
Post or offer a task

Need help moving a sofa? Got a free hour and a drill? Post a task or browse open ones near you.

3
Exchange credits, not cash

When help is given, credits change hands. Use yours to get help back — or save them for a rainy day.

4
Build your reputation

Ratings are left by both sides. The more you help, the stronger your standing in the community.
