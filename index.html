How it works

Three steps to
getting things done

✓ 1
Post what you need

Describe your task in plain language. Takes 60 seconds. Set a credit offer and a rough time — that's it.

✓ 2
A neighbor picks it up

Real people from your area see your task and offer to help. Chat, agree on details, get it done. No middleman.

✓ 3
Pay it forward

Earn credits by helping others. Spend them when you need a hand. The more you give, the more you get back.
